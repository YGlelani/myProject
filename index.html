Programs We Offer

Our programs are designed to empower learners in our community to achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

Career Guidance Drive

Helps high school learners explore career options, receive mentorship, and make informed decisions about their future.

University Applicatins Drive

Assists matric learners with university applications, bursaries, and scholarships to ensure access to higher education.

Agricultural Career Motivation Event

Promotes agricultural education, training, and agribusiness opportunities to support food security and rural development.

Sanitary Pads Campaign

Provides free sanitary products and menstrual health education to keep girls in school and promote gender equality.

Literacy Program Bookathon

Encourages reading and literacy through book donations, reading sessions, and tutoring for learners.

NSFAS Applications Drive

Helping students apply for NSFAS funding by providing guidance, document assistance, and support.

About Us

Ikusasalentsha is a non-profit organization that seeks to assist matriculants with online applications to colleges, universities, and bursaries. The organization also provides career guidance, study support, and provides health talks.
NPO currently operates in the rural town of Bergville, located at the foothills of the Drakensberg Mountains
